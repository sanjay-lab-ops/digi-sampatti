Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026 · Last Updated: April 3, 2026
DigiSampatti is a private PropTech SaaS platform. We do not sell, share, or license your personal data to any third party for commercial purposes. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

1. Who We Are

DigiSampatti ("we", "our", "the platform") is a private property intelligence SaaS platform operated by DigiSampatti (Startup India Registration No. IN-0326-9427JD), based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. We provide property verification reports, due diligence analytics, government service application assistance, and related proptech services through our Android application.

2. Data We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Name, mobile number, email address — for account creation
  • Aadhaar number (last 4 digits only) — for identity verification via OTP only; full Aadhaar is never stored
  • Property details: survey number, district, taluk — to generate verification reports
  • Documents you upload: RTC, EC, sale deed — for AI-based OCR analysis only
  • Payment information — processed entirely by Cashfree; we do not store card or UPI details

Information collected automatically:

  • Device type, OS version, app version — for crash diagnostics
  • Usage analytics — which features are used (anonymised, no personal identifiers)
  • GPS location — only when you explicitly use the "Scan This Land" feature; never collected in background

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate property verification reports using publicly accessible government data sources
  • To assist you in filing government service applications (Mutation, Khata, EC) on your behalf
  • To send you property alerts and application status notifications
  • To process payments via our payment gateway partner (Cashfree)
  • To improve the accuracy of our AI contradiction engine and fraud detection system
  • To comply with applicable Indian law, including IT Act 2000 and DPDP Act 2023

4. Government Portal Data Access

DigiSampatti accesses the following publicly available government portals on your behalf as a citizen right, using no special licensing or government MOU:

  • Bhoomi (Karnataka Department of Revenue)
  • KAVERI / IGRS (Karnataka Registration and Stamps)
  • CERSAI (RBI Central Registry)
  • eCourts (National Judicial Data Grid)
  • BBMP (Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike)
  • RERA Karnataka
  • Benami Transactions IT Portal (Income Tax Department)

All data retrieved from these portals is displayed to you as-is. DigiSampatti does not modify, alter, or fabricate any government data. Verification reports reflect the state of public records at the time of query.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Cashfree Payments: Transaction data for payment processing
  • Anthropic Claude API: Document images sent for OCR analysis — anonymised, no personal identifiers sent
  • Legal experts on our panel: Only when you explicitly request an expert consultation and consent to share your report
  • Law enforcement: Only if required by a valid court order under Indian law

6. Data Security

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3
  • Documents uploaded for analysis are deleted from our servers within 24 hours
  • We do not store full Aadhaar numbers — only the last 4 digits after OTP verification
  • Payment data is never stored on our servers — fully handled by Cashfree PCI-DSS infrastructure
  • Digital signatures in verification reports use SHA-256 hashing and are tamper-evident

7. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Grievance: File a complaint with our Data Protection Officer
  • Nomination: Nominate a person to exercise data rights on your behalf

To exercise any right, email: privacy@digisampatti.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The DigiSampatti Android application does not use cookies. We use anonymised Firebase Analytics for crash reporting and feature usage — no personal identifiers are linked to analytics data. You can opt out of analytics in the app settings.

9. Children's Privacy

DigiSampatti is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us at privacy@digisampatti.in and we will delete the account immediately.

10. Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Verification reports: Stored in your account for 2 years, then automatically deleted unless renewed.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian tax law (GST records).
  • Uploaded documents: Deleted within 24 hours of report generation.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of material changes via push notification and in-app alert at least 7 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of the app after that date constitutes acceptance.
