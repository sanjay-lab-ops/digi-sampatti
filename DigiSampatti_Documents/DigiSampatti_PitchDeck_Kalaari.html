The Problem
Every property transaction in India is a leap of faith.
No one verifies. Fraud is the default.
📄
Forged Documents
RTC owner name altered, fake e-stamp paper, EC showing clean while mortgage exists in CERSAI. All undetectable by eye.
⚖️
Hidden Litigation
Seller hides active court cases. eCourts isn't checked manually. Buyers discover the dispute after registration.
⏳
Opaque Process
Mutation takes 30 days by law. Takes 2 years in practice. No tracking, no escalation, no recourse. Buyers stuck.
The data to prevent all of this exists — in 7 public government portals. The problem is no one built the automation layer. Until now.

Private PropTech SaaS.
