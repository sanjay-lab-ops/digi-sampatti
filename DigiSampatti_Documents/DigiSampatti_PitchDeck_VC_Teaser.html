The Product

Three Ways In. One Complete Answer.

Camera. GPS. Type. Any entry point. Same complete 7-portal result.

📷 Camera — Scan the Document

Photograph any property paper — RTC, sale deed, mutation certificate. OCR extracts owner name, survey number, village, taluk, district automatically. User confirms. Full check runs. Works for farmers who cannot type survey numbers.

📍 GPS — Stand on the Land

No document needed. Stand on the property. Tap once. GPS coordinates query Karnataka's Bhoomi cadastral GIS layer → survey number identified automatically → 7-portal verification begins. Works even for buyers who have no paperwork yet.

⌨️ Type — Enter Survey Number

Fastest path for users who know the property ID. One entry, full report. Used by urban buyers, NRIs verifying remotely, banks processing loans, and real estate professionals checking multiple properties.

Government portal fees (Bhoomi ₹15–25, IGRS ₹30–50, CERSAI ₹10) are paid by DigiSampatti on behalf of the citizen and included in the ₹99 service fee. Government revenue is fully maintained — and increases as more citizens access portals through DigiSampatti than would have individually.

Trust Architecture

Mistake-Proof by Design. Better With Every Citizen.

Five protection layers. Three feedback loops. The AI that never stops learning.

Five Protection Layers

Layer 1 — Government Is Always Source of Truth

DigiSampatti never creates data. Every verdict is based on live government portal data. If government data is wrong, that is a government correction issue — DigiSampatti guides the citizen to the official rectification process.

Layer 2 — SAFE / CAUTION / DO NOT BUY. Never Absolute.

Even a SAFE verdict says "recommend lawyer review before final payment." DigiSampatti is a decision support tool — never a guarantee. Reduces liability while maximising citizen protection.

Layer 3 — Property Confirmation Before Check

Citizen confirms owner name, village, taluk, area before report generates. Eliminates wrong survey number errors — the most common single point of failure.

Layer 4 — Expert Council Review on Every CAUTION

Every CAUTION verdict triggers a lawyer review offer. Human judgment is the final filter before any transaction decision. Expert Council members earn per review.

Layer 5 — 48-Hour Dispute Resolution

Citizens dispute any report within 30 days. Manual review by expert council member within 48 hours. Written correction issued. Professional indemnity insurance carried.

Three Feedback Loops

Loop 1 — Citizen Outcome

90 days after SAFE verdict: "Did the transaction complete cleanly?" Any issue found post-purchase trains the AI on what was missed. Real-world outcomes, not simulated tests.

Loop 2 — Community Fraud Reports

Citizens report brokers, disputed properties, area patterns. Verified reports added to Karnataka fraud map. One Kolar report protects thousands of future buyers across the state.

Loop 3 — Expert Council Corrections

When a lawyer reviewing a CAUTION case finds something the AI missed or over-flagged, the correction is fed back. Lawyers improve the AI. The AI improves the lawyers' efficiency. Continuous.

Three feedback sources simultaneously: citizen outcomes, community intelligence, legal expert corrections. No competitor has all three. Each loop makes the others more powerful.
