Two separate things:

Raw portal documents (Bhoomi RTC, IGRS EC PDFs retrieved from government) — permanently deleted from DigiSampatti servers after 30 days. This is DPDP Act compliance. We have no reason to hold government data longer than needed.

What Happens If DigiSampatti Makes a Mistake

Five layers of protection — because a wrong verdict on a ₹40 lakh purchase is unacceptable

Protection 1 — Government Is Always the Source of Truth

DigiSampatti does not create data. It reads from official government portals. If government says clear, we say clear. The source of truth is always the government. If government data is wrong, that is a government data correction issue — we guide the citizen to the official Revenue Department correction process.

Protection 2 — Three-Level Verdict, Never Absolute

We never say "this property is 100% safe to buy." We say SAFE, CAUTION, or DO NOT BUY. Even a SAFE verdict comes with: "We recommend consulting a lawyer before finalizing payment." DigiSampatti is a decision support tool — not a legal guarantee. We make risks visible, not invisible.

Protection 3 — Two-Step Property Confirmation

Before generating any report, the citizen must confirm: "Is this the correct property?" We show owner name, village, taluk, area, survey number. The citizen confirms. Only then do we proceed. This prevents wrong survey number mistakes — the most common human error in any property transaction.

Protection 4 — Lawyer Review on Every CAUTION

Every CAUTION verdict automatically offers a verified Karnataka lawyer review through our partner network. The lawyer's human judgment is the final filter before the citizen makes any payment or signs any agreement. DigiSampatti refers clients to lawyers — it never replaces them on complex cases.

Protection 5 — Dispute Resolution Within 48 Hours

If a citizen believes our report is incorrect, they raise a dispute. Our team reviews the original government portal records manually within 48 hours and issues a correction with explanation. We carry professional indemnity insurance for report errors. The citizen is never left without recourse. If we are wrong, we fix it — and we are insured for it.

Comparison: A lawyer also makes mistakes. A bank officer also misses encumbrances. No system is perfect. DigiSampatti adds more checks, more layers, and more recourse than any current alternative — while being 150x cheaper than a lawyer.
