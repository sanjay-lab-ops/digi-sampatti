Slide 03

The India-Native Problem

This problem exists only in India. And only now can it be solved.
A farmer in Tumkur buys 2 acres of land — life savings of ₹40 lakhs. Six months later: court summons. The land had 3 prior encumbrances. He never knew. Nobody told him.

Why It Happens

Property verification requires 6 separate government portals, different logins, different formats, 3–5 days, and ₹15,000 lawyer fees. 90% of buyers skip it entirely and buy blind.

Why Now

Karnataka launched Kaveri 3.0 (March 2026, ₹65Cr). Auto-mutation live statewide — 35L+ records automated. Bhoomi 8.0 announced. Government is building the rails. DigiSampatti is the train.

This is not a metro problem. Karnataka alone has 1.2 crore property transactions per year. Farmers. First-time buyers. NRIs. None of them have a product built for them.
