Fraud Detection

8 defects.
Every one blocked.

These are the exact frauds that cost Karnataka property buyers crores every year. DigiSampatti catches each one automatically before your money leaves your account.

8
Fraud types blocked
automatically on every report
1
Forged RTC Owner Name
Broker prints RTC with altered owner name. DigiSampatti reads the embedded QR code and compares directly with Bhoomi portal.
→ QR mismatch = FORGERY alert. Instant.
2
Hidden Bank Mortgage
EC shows clean — no encumbrance. But a mortgage exists in CERSAI (RBI registry). Contradiction caught automatically.
→ EC + CERSAI contradiction = DO NOT BUY.
3
Fake e-Stamp Paper
Reused or duplicate stamp paper UIN. Every e-stamp verified against SHCIL registry. Duplicate flagged immediately.
→ Duplicate UIN = FRAUD. Cannot reuse.
4
Hidden Court Cases
Seller conceals active litigation. eCourts searched by owner name — finds cases even if not filed under the survey number.
→ Active case = CAUTION with case details.
5
Benami Property
Property held benami — real owner is someone else. Income Tax benami registry checked automatically on every report.
→ Benami flag = immediate DO NOT BUY.
6
Seller Identity Fraud
Impersonation — someone sells property they don't own. Aadhaar OTP + face match required. Name must match RTC owner.
→ Mismatch = registration blocked.
7
Silent Property Transfer
Someone transfers your property without your knowledge. 24/7 monitoring on all 7 portals. Any change triggers instant notification.
→ Change detected = SMS + push in 30 mins.
8
Broker Cash Advance Fraud
Broker collects advance and disappears. DigiSampatti escrow holds advance until verification clears. Auto-refund if seller defaults.
→ Escrow = no cash changes hands unsafely.
