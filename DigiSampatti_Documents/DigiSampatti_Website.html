The Problem

Property Fraud Happens Because Nobody Checks

Every year, thousands of Karnataka buyers lose crores to fraud that a 90-second check would have caught. The data exists. It's public. No one built the check.

📄
RTC Owner Name Altered
Broker prints a fake RTC with altered owner name. Buyer has no way to verify. Sale deed is registered. Buyer discovers fraud 2 years later in court.
🏦
Hidden Bank Mortgage
EC shows clean. But the property has an active mortgage in CERSAI (RBI registry). Buyer pays full price. Bank forecloses. Buyer loses everything.
⚖️
Active Court Case Hidden
Seller doesn't mention a civil dispute. It's in eCourts. Title transfer is later set aside by court order. Buyer's investment gone.
🖨️
Fake e-Stamp Paper
Stamp paper UIN is reused from a previous transaction. Undetectable by eye. SHCIL portal has the truth — nobody checks it manually.
⏳
Mutation Stuck for Years
Property registered. Mutation application filed. Officer sits on file. No escalation mechanism. Buyer can't get bank loan. Property limbo for 2+ years.
💸
Bribe Demanded at Every Step
VA, Revenue Inspector, ARO — every officer demands informal payment to move files. No recourse. No transparency. No deadline enforcement.

Mistake-Proof

8 Defects. All Arrested.

Every known fraud type in Karnataka property transactions — detected and blocked automatically. Every degree of freedom locked.

01
Broker alters RTC owner name
QR code verified against Bhoomi portal directly. Mismatch = FORGERY alert. Cannot alter a QR without the government's private key.
02
EC clean but hidden bank mortgage
CERSAI checked simultaneously. EC + CERSAI contradiction = automatic DO NOT BUY. No single document trusted alone.
03
Fake e-Stamp paper (reused UIN)
SHCIL UIN verified. Duplicate UIN = FRAUD alert. Used stamps flagged. Impossible to reuse on our platform.
04
Court case hidden by seller
eCourts searched by owner name (not just survey number). Finds cases even if not formally linked to the property.
05
Broker takes advance and disappears
DS Escrow holds advance until verification clears. Auto-refunded if seller defaults. No cash changes hands outside platform.
06
Mutation rejected, buyer stuck for years
DS Mutation Service reads rejection reason, prepares corrected application, refiles. Government SLA tracked. Auto-escalated to Tahsildar.
07
Property transferred without your knowledge
Property Alert Monitor watches all 7 portals 24/7. Any change = SMS + push in 30 minutes. Freeze via DigiSampatti immediately.
08
Seller identity fraud (impersonation)
Aadhaar OTP + face match at registration. Name on Aadhaar must match RTC owner name. Discrepancy = manual review before any listing.
