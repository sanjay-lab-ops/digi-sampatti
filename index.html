The Problem

Most Property Frauds Happen Because Buyers Don't Know What to Check

Government portals exist — but they require legal knowledge to interpret. Most buyers rely on brokers who have a conflict of interest.

🔍

Hidden Encumbrances

Loans, liabilities, or court orders on the property that sellers hide from buyers.

🏛️

Government Claims

Land acquisition notices or ceiling violations that make a property legally unsafe.

📋

Fake RERA Projects

Builders selling apartments in unregistered or cancelled RERA projects.

👥

Co-ownership Disputes

Multiple heirs, undivided family property, or disputed succession — invisible without proper records check.
